🎤 The Xaviera Sound

A genre-fluid artist blending Neo-Soul, Hip-Hop, and R&B with raw storytelling. Each song is a chapter of resilience, spirituality, and self-discovery—music that doesn't just sound good, it feels true.

"Alhamdulillah"
A gratitude anthem inspired by the Arabic term "Alhamdulillah" which means "Thanks to God"—celebrating divine blessings and spiritual journey
"Takotsubo"
Exploring broken heart syndrome—the Japanese word for emotional collapse and the strength found in rebuilding alone
"Placid" ft. Jxt Clement
Amapiano roots meet defiant truth: "You couldn't even be me if you wanted to"—standing firm as the truth that hates to see you coming
"Barakah"
Divine blessings made audible—a reminder to recognize how covered you are, giving gratitude for every hidden mercy
"Yusuf"
Personal betrayal echoing Prophet Yusuf's story—when loved ones become the pit you're left in, and survival becomes your testimony
"You & I (Intro)" - Featured Artist
Special feature collaboration track - a soulful introduction to musical connection and shared artistry
